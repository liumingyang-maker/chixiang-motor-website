Preguntas Frecuentes

Can you confirm exact model availability?

Yes. Send the target model, displacement, quantity and destination market. The sales team will confirm stock, lead time and matching spare parts.

Can the engine be customized?

OEM logo, casing color, packaging, starting system and market-specific configuration can be discussed for qualified bulk orders.

Can spare parts ship with engines?

Yes. Fast-moving service parts can be bundled with engine orders to support local after-sales service.
