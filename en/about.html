About Chixiang Motors

Quality-first, reputation-driven. Engineering excellence since 2003.

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Chongqing Chixiang Motorcycle Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is a leading manufacturer specializing in motorcycle and cargo tricycle engine parts and complete engines. Headquartered in Hangu Town, Jiulongpo District, Chongqing — adjacent to the Chengyu Expressway for efficient logistics — we serve markets across China and export to Southeast Asia, Pakistan, the Middle East, Africa, and Central Asia.

Our main products span motorcycle and tricycle engine components from 70cc to CG350cc series, including water-cooled models. We provide customized solutions such as engines with built-in reverse gear — ideally suited for the cargo operations popular in Central Asian markets. Products are already receiving strong positive feedback from local clients in the region.

Our factory is fully equipped with an automated engine assembly line, two testing laboratories, two exhaust gas analyzers, and eddy current dynamometers. With a monthly production capacity of 3,000 engines and a 99% first-pass rate, we ensure stable supply and consistent product quality.
